About me and this website.

Hello! I'm Sobful, or as my online friends call me, Sob.

I started this website back in 2022, inspired by the game MapleStory. My fascination has always been with numbers, and I found myself particularly drawn to the game's RNG mechanics. This curiosity led me to create a tool that could help both myself and others make informed decisions and set realistic expectations in the game. That's how this website was born.

Over the years, I've expanded the site to include a variety of tools, each reflecting my love for numbers and coding. You'll find all these tools here on the website, each crafted with the intention to demystify complex game mechanics and bring clarity to players.

Nothing makes me happier than knowing that my tools have helped you in some way, or that the content I made on YouTube made you laugh. If you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me by joining my Discord channel. I'd love to hear from you!
